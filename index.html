  • Online: www.va.gov/health-care/apply
  • By Phone: 877-222-VETS (8387)
  • By Mail: Mail VA Form 10-10EZ to:
    Health Eligibility Center
    PO Box 5207
    Janesville, WI 53547-5207
  • In Person: At any VA health care facility.
